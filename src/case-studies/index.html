Cruise Guide

Marlborough District Council

Cruise Guide puts the Marlborough Sounds in the palm of your hand. Media Suite joined forces with Marlborough District Council to provide the public with the best possible tool for navigation and recreation in the beautiful Sounds. We combined 25 open data sets with offline capability and expert local knowledge, all in a free app for Android and iPhone, and a website.

Transport for Christchurch

Christchurch City Council/NZTA

In the wake of the Canterbury earthquakes, Media Suite was engaged by the Christchurch Transport Operations Centre (A joint venture between the New Zealand Transport Authority, Christchurch City Council other local councils) to develop a long-term, web-based communication channel for the travelling public in the greater Christchurch region.
